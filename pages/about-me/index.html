About Me

My name is Young. I am an aspiring Data Scientist currently enrolled at Metis. At Metis, the first important thing I learned about Data Science is not what one can do with data, but what story one can tell others using data. As someone who comes from a technical background that required little story telling (Electrical Engineering, although some technical writing is required), that sticks to me as the crux of my journey into becoming a Data Scientist.

Hence, my goal of the blog is to explore and share with the world the findings in my data science journey, and most importantly, what story I can tell you about it. I hope to bring more exciting content in the future!

My spare time is taken up with consuming sports, especially European Soccer, NBA and the NFL, and (sometimes) tequila.
